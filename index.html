News

This website is simply about the importance of distinguishing the difference between real and fake news. Sometimes it's hard to tell because some fake news websites and pages are replicas of the real ones, but there are definitely differences What we do, is we create awareness by adding videos about exactly how to tell the difference and what not to do to make mistakes of thinking you have entered into a real website, and also to give you information about how fake news are impacting the world nowadays.

About

We are an organisation, City of Westminster College, and we have created a website to spread awareness of the topic of Fake News. Not alot of organisations talk about the importance of knowing the difference between fake and real news and how it's spreading radidly to young people, in which they will get information that has been mislead or misinterpreted. Our job as an organisation, especially a college for young adults, is to teach them how to know what news articles thay are reading is fake or not, so that they dont go reading false information.
